When asked to describe itself GPT said:

> "I am an AI language model trained by OpenAI. I am designed to assist users with a wide range of tasks, including answering questions, providing information, and engaging in general conversation. I am able to understand and generate human-like text in a variety of languages. I do not have personal feelings or opinions, and my responses are based solely on the information that I have been trained on. My primary goal is to be helpful and provide accurate and useful information to users."
